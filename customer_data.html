Customer Segmentation for a Digital Banking App

Overview

We analyzed three years of transaction data from a digital banking app to segment customers using PCA and K-means clustering, uncovering behavioral patterns that enable personalized offers to increase customer lifetime value.

Core Features:

  • Aggregated and engineered customer-level features (frequency, spend, variability, recency)
  • Identified five distinct customer clusters with unique behavioral profiles
  • Created personas and developed targeted business recommendations for each group
